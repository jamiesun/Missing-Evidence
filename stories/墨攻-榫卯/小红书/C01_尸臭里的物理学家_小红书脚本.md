# 小红书视频脚本：C01 尸臭里的物理学家

## 1. 文案 (Narration)
> **建议时长**：40s
> **语气**：冷淡、疲惫，像是在念一张过期的账单

“这时候你还不用知道我是谁。
你只需要知道，在那堆烂泥和尸臭里，我弄丢了眼镜，也弄丢了那个即使在实验室里也会为了数据哭鼻子的自己。
也是在那天黄昏，我才明白物理学的真正奥义。
杠杆原理救不了世界，但它能把一个想杀你的人，变成一条漂亮的抛物线。
这一刀下去，文明成了沉没成本。
活下来的不是马静仪。
叫马六。”

---

## 2. 画面背板描述 (Visual Prompt)
> **工具**：Midjourney / Stable Diffusion
> **风格**：王家卫 / 杜可风 (Wong Kar-wai / Christopher Doyle)

**Prompt:**
```text
Abstract cinematic background, Wong Kar-wai visual style, Christopher Doyle cinematography.

A mood-drenched, atmospheric scene at twilight. High ISO, film grain.
Foreground: Extremely blurred silhouette of dry, tangled tree roots or wild grass blocking the lower frame, creating a voyeuristic, claustrophobic feel.
Middle Ground: A sharp, textured focus on a crude, blood-stained wooden lever (a thick branch pivoting on a rock) resting in the mud. The wood looks wet and heavy.
Background: Deep, out-of-focus gloom. A faint, sickeningly warm orange light (sunset) fights against a suffocating cool teal mist.

Lighting: Strong Chiaroscuro. The lever is rim-lit by the dying sun, while the rest of the world is swallowed by heavy, oily shadows.
Atmosphere: Humidity, despair, wet soil texture.
No people, no faces, just the silent evidence of violence. --ar 3:4
```

---

## 3. 微动建议 (Motion)
> **工具**：Runway Gen-2 / Luma Dream Machine

*   **Step-printing Effect:** 极慢的抽帧/残影效果（王家卫标志性视觉），让画面有一种“时间变得粘稠”的感觉。
*   **Subtle Light Leak:** 画面边缘有一丝非常微弱的暖色漏光，缓慢地忽明忽暗，像破损的胶片。

---

## 4. 音效 / 氛围建议 (Sound)
> **工具**：Suno v3/v3.5 (Custom Mode)

**设置:**
*   **Toggle:** 开启 Instrumental (纯音乐)
*   **Style of Music:** `Dark Ambient, Cinematic Drone, Lo-fi, Tape Hiss, Slow, Minimalist, Eerie, Melancholic, Low Frequency, No Percussion`

**Lyrics (用于引导氛围):**
```text
[Intro]
(Hollow wind blowing)
(Low throbbing hum)

[Verse]
(Silence)
(Faint buzzing)

[Outro]
(Fading into tape hiss)
```

**后期建议:**
音量调低至 -10dB ~ -15dB，作为文案的衬底。
